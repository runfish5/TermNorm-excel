Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 21, 2024

This Privacy Policy describes how TermNorm ("Software," "we," "our") handles information when you use our Microsoft Excel add-in. Your privacy is important to us.

Summary: TermNorm processes all data locally on your computer. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data to our servers. Third-party AI services may receive your queries when you use LLM features.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

TermNorm does not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, address)
  • Your Excel spreadsheet contents
  • Usage analytics or telemetry
  • Cookies or tracking data
  • Device identifiers

2. Local Data Processing

All data processing occurs locally:

Component Location Data Stored
Backend Server Your local machine (localhost) Session data, match cache, configuration
Match Database Local JSON files Previously matched terms (for faster lookups)
Configuration Local files Column mappings, settings preferences

3. Third-Party Services

Important: When you use the LLM research feature, your query text is sent to third-party AI providers.

TermNorm may connect to these third-party services:

3.1 LLM Providers

When using AI-powered matching, your search queries may be sent to:

  • Groq - Privacy Policy
  • OpenAI - Privacy Policy

3.2 Web Search APIs

When researching terminology, queries may be sent to:

  • Brave Search - Privacy Policy
  • DuckDuckGo - Privacy Policy
  • SearXNG (self-hosted instances)

Please review these third-party privacy policies to understand how they handle your data.

4. Data You Control

You have full control over your data:

  • Delete cache: Remove local match database files at any time
  • Clear history: Delete session logs from the backend folder
  • Disable features: Use only local matching without LLM/web features
  • Uninstall: Removing the add-in removes all associated local data

5. Data Security

Since data is processed locally:

  • Your data never leaves your network (except for explicit LLM/search queries)
  • No cloud storage or remote databases are used
  • Security depends on your local machine and network security

6. Children's Privacy

TermNorm is designed for professional use and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
